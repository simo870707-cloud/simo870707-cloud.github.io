Privacy Policy

The Living EDIT · Last updated 30 June 2026

The Living EDIT (“the app”, “we”) is a calm daily edit of surprising facts for home and mind. We care about keeping your data minimal and private. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • On your device: your name (optional), your interests, your saved facts and preferences are stored locally on your device using your browser’s storage. We do not see this unless you choose to back it up or sign in.
  • If you create an account (optional): your email address and the data you choose to sync (saved facts, preferences) are stored securely with our hosting provider so they survive across devices and reinstalls.
  • Subscriptions: purchases of The Living EDIT+ are handled entirely by Google Play. We receive confirmation that you have an active subscription, but we never see your card or payment details.
  • We do not show ads, sell your data, or use third-party advertising or tracking.

How we use it

To run the app: to remember your saved facts and preferences, to personalise the facts you see, and to grant access to The Living EDIT+ if you subscribe.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data. We use trusted service providers only to operate the app:

  • Google Play — to process subscriptions.
  • Our database/hosting provider — to store account data if you sign in.

Your choices

You can export or delete your data at any time from within the app. If you have an account and want it deleted, contact us and we will remove your data.

Children

The Living EDIT is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the date above.
